The Lidraughts forum is organized into categories covering general discussion, draughts strategy, announcements, and team-specific boards. All registered users can read and post in public categories.

## Forum categories

Visit [lidraughts.org/forum](https://lidraughts.org/forum) to see the full list of categories. Each category shows the title, description, number of topics, and the most recent post.

Categories include global boards visible to all users as well as private boards that are only accessible to members of specific teams.

<Info>
  The forum is not available to users who have Kids mode enabled.
</Info>

## Recent posts

The **Recent posts** page at [lidraughts.org/forum/recent](https://lidraughts.org/forum/recent) shows the latest posts across all categories you have access to. Use this page to catch up on active discussions without navigating category by category.

## Searching the forum

Use the search page at [lidraughts.org/forum/search](https://lidraughts.org/forum/search) to find posts by keyword. Enter your search term and the results are paginated.

```text
/forum/search?text=draughts+tactics
```

Search covers all public categories. Staff forum categories are only included in results for users with staff access.

## Creating a topic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a category">
    Navigate to the category where you want to post, for example `/forum/general-draughts-discussion`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New topic">
    Click the **New topic** button. This takes you to the topic form at `/forum/<category>/form`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your topic">
    Enter a title and the body text. Both are required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the captcha">
    Solve the draughts captcha to prevent spam.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Post** to publish. You are redirected to the new topic page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Topic creation is rate-limited to 2 topics per 5 minutes per IP address. Repeated submissions above this limit will be rejected.
</Warning>

## Posting a reply

Open any topic and scroll to the reply form at the bottom of the last page. Type your reply and click **Post**.

- Replies are rate-limited to 4 posts per 5 minutes per IP address.
- You can edit your own post after submitting.
- Closed or archived topics do not show the reply form.

<Note>
  A topic is considered **archived** once it has been inactive for an extended period. You can still read it but cannot add new replies.
</Note>

## Editing a post

You can edit your own posts after publishing. The edit form is available on each post and submits to `/forum/post/<id>`. Changes are applied immediately.

## Reactions

You can add emoji reactions to forum posts. Click the reaction button on a post to toggle a reaction on or off. Reactions are visible to all readers.

## Forum rules

The forum follows the Lidraughts terms of service. Key expectations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stay on topic">
    Post in the category that best matches your subject. Off-topic threads may be moved or closed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Be respectful">
    Personal attacks, harassment, and hate speech are not allowed. Treat other users the way you would want to be treated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No spam">
    Do not post duplicate messages, flood a thread with repeated content, or advertise unrelated services.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No illegal content">
    Sharing illegal material, piracy links, or content that violates applicable law is prohibited.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Moderation

Moderation actions are available to users with the appropriate permissions.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Close a topic" icon="lock">
    Toggle a topic open or closed. Closed topics are read-only. Available to category moderators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hide a topic" icon="eye-slash">
    Hide a topic from public view. Requires the `ModerateForum` permission.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sticky a topic" icon="thumbtack">
    Pin a topic to the top of the category. Available to category moderators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete a post" icon="trash">
    Remove an individual post. Available to category moderators.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Moderator actions summary

| Action | Route | Who can do it |
|---|---|---|
| Close / reopen topic | `POST /forum/:categ/:slug/close` | Category moderator |
| Hide / unhide topic | `POST /forum/:categ/:slug/hide` | `ModerateForum` role |
| Sticky / unsticky topic | `POST /forum/:categ/:slug/sticky` | Category moderator |
| Delete a post | `POST /forum/:categ/delete/:id` | Category moderator |

## Topic participants

You can see the list of usernames who have posted in a topic by requesting `/forum/participants/<topicId>`. This returns a JSON array of usernames sorted alphabetically.

## Team forums

Each team has its own private forum category visible only to members. Navigate to the team page and click the forum link. Posting in a team forum follows the same flow as the global forum, but write access is restricted to team members.

See [Teams](/community/teams) for more information about team management.
